# Salla Call Center Operations Analytics

## 📌 Project Overview
This repository contains an end-to-end data analysis project dedicated to optimizing the performance, staffing, and customer satisfaction of Salla's Call Center operations. By analyzing structured call data, this project delivers strategic growth insights and interactive dashboards to assist stakeholders in driving operational efficiency.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Skills Demonstrated
* **SQL:** Advanced querying, data cleaning, aggregating key performance indicators (KPIs), and time-series analysis.
* **Power BI:** Data modeling (Star Schema), DAX measures, and interactive dashboard design.
* **Data Storytelling & Business Intelligence:** Translating raw operational metrics into strategic business recommendations.

---

## 📊 Key Insights & Business Recommendations

### 1. Response & Resolution Times
* **Insight:** Our analysis identified significant bottlenecks during peak business hours, causing spikes in average handling and waiting times.
* **Recommendation:** Implement a dynamic shifting schedule for agents to align staffing levels with historical peak call volumes.

### 2. Customer Satisfaction (CSAT) Optimization
* **Insight:** A direct correlation was found between long wait times and drop-offs in CSAT scores.
* **Recommendation:** Introduce automated self-service IVR menus for common queries to reduce the load on live agents.

---
